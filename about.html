About

I’m Chandni, a designer and researcher working at the intersections of labor, AI, and digital storytelling. I care deeply about thoughtful technology, ethical design, and the future of work.

Right now, I’m writing The Thoughtful Technologist, designing AI-powered tools for productivity, and connecting artists with garment workers in Tuticorin.

When I’m not working, I write about rain, language, and belonging.
