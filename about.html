About

I’m Chandni, a designer and researcher working at the intersection of labor, AI, and digital storytelling.

Currently, I’m writing The Thoughtful Technologist, designing AI-powered tools for productivity, and connecting artists with garment workers in Tuticorin.

When I’m not working, I’m writing about rain, language, and belonging. You can reach me at hello@chandninaidu.com.
